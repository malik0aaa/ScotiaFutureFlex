# ScotiaFutureFlex 🏦

> A Scotiabank Digital Banking Solution — Built for [case]Hacks 2026

## Overview

ScotiaFutureFlex is a feature concept for Scotiabank's mobile app that converts existing depositors (aged 18–34) into first-time investors — starting with their very next transaction.

The core insight: **41% of Gen Z and Millennials already have a TFSA but aren't investing inside it.** The barrier isn't money — it's confidence and clarity. ScotiaFutureFlex removes both.

## The Problem

Scotiabank has millions of young Canadians as banking customers. Their paycheques arrive here. Their savings sit here. But when it comes to investing, those same customers open Wealthsimple on another tab — because Scotiabank never made the first step feel easy.

- **41%** of Gen Z & Millennials have a TFSA but aren't investing inside it
- **43%** of younger Canadians don't know where to begin
- **$1 trillion** in wealth is transferring to younger Canadians by 2026

## Our Solution

Three features that close the gap from depositor to investor:

### 01 — Round-Up Investing
Every purchase is rounded up to the nearest dollar. The spare change is automatically invested into the user's chosen portfolio. No minimum, no manual decision required.

### 02 — Instant Starter Portfolio
A 60-second onboarding quiz recommends the right account (TFSA, FHSA, or RRSP) in plain language. One tap opens it. No jargon, no branch visit required.

### 03 — Rewards & Streaks
Users earn XP for contributions, unlock badges, and climb leaderboards. Investing becomes a habit loop — not a one-time event.
